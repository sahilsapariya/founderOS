# FounderOS — Product Vision

**Version:** 1.0
**Status:** Draft
**Owner:** Sahil Sapariya

---

# 1. Vision

FounderOS is an AI-powered operating system built for founders, builders, freelancers, and software engineers who manage multiple projects simultaneously.

Rather than replacing existing tools, FounderOS becomes the single place a user opens every morning to understand the complete health of their work, business, finances, and goals.

The dashboard should answer one simple question:

> **"What should I focus on today?"**

Everything in the product exists to help answer that question.

---

# 2. Mission

To help founders make better decisions by bringing together projects, tasks, meetings, goals, finances, notes, AI insights, and integrations into one unified workspace.

FounderOS should reduce context switching while increasing clarity and execution.

---

# 3. Problem Statement

Modern founders use many disconnected tools:

* Jira
* GitHub
* Google Calendar
* Google Drive
* Gmail
* Notion
* Slack
* Spreadsheets

Information becomes scattered across multiple platforms.

The result is:

* Constant context switching
* Forgotten tasks
* Poor visibility into project health
* No single source of truth
* Difficulty deciding what deserves attention

FounderOS solves this by providing a centralized command center.

# 5. Product Philosophy

FounderOS is **not** a task manager.

FounderOS is **not** another Notion.

FounderOS is **not** another Jira.

FounderOS is a decision-support system.

Every screen should help users understand:

* Where they are
* What matters
* What is blocked
* What should happen next

---

# 6. Core Principles

## One Dashboard

Users should understand the health of their entire business from one screen.

---

## Information Dense

The interface should present rich information without feeling cluttered.

---

## AI First

AI is an assistant, not a chatbot.

It should actively organize, summarize, prioritize, and recommend.

---

## Modular

Every feature is an independent module.

Examples:

* Projects
* Finance
* Calendar
* Goals
* GitHub
* Notes

Modules should expose:

* Dashboard Widget
* Detailed Page
* Quick Actions
* AI Actions

---

## Beautiful

The product should feel premium.

Design inspiration:

* Linear
* Vercel
* Apple
* Raycast

---

## Fast

Everything should feel instant.

* Keyboard shortcuts
* Command palette
* Fast search
* Optimistic UI
* Smooth animations

# 9. Success Metrics

A successful FounderOS user should:

* Open the dashboard every morning.
* Spend less time switching between apps.
* Complete more planned work each week.
* Have clear visibility into project progress.
* Know whether they are on track financially.

---

# 10. Future Vision

FounderOS should evolve into a platform where founders can manage their entire business.

Future capabilities may include:

* Team collaboration
* CRM
* Client management
* Invoicing
* Time tracking
* AI agents
* Email management
* Knowledge base
* Mobile applications
* Marketplace for integrations

---

# 11. Non-Goals (MVP)

The first version will intentionally avoid:

* Full accounting
* Payroll
* HR management
* ERP functionality
* Complex workflow automation
* Jira replacement
* Slack replacement
* Full document management

These may become integrations or future modules, but they are not required for the initial release.
